Environment

The environment is the complex of physical, geographical, and biological conditions surrounding a person or other living organism. The environment affects the lives of people and society. The environment is linked to humanity and 13,000,000 people die every year due to environmental pollution. Because of this, protecting the environment is protecting human lives now and in the future

Ecology studies the fundamentals of environmental protection. Studying the impact of progress on the environment (biosphere) is one of the important problems of our time. The purpose of the study is to protect the environment.
